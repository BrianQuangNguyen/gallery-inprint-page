About Us

Who are we?

Gallery Inprint is a print house and art gallery run by Peter McLennan and based in Ontario, Canada. Founded in 2006, we've helped dozens of clients preserve their most meaningful memories through our photography. Whether capturing life's biggest milestones or its everyday wonders that are so often overlooked, our mission is to celebrate all the beauty the world has to offer.


What do we do?

Here at Gallery Inprint, we offer a variety of services, including photography and professional photo shoots, printing services, and a curated collection of ready-made prints available for purchase. Check out our Shop page to see what we have to offer right now!
